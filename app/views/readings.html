Reading Seminar led by Emeritus Prof John Clark, Prof Adrian Vickers and Dr Stephen Whiteman

5 February 2015, 1300-1500

Key texts for discussion:

  • George Kubler, The shape of time: remarks on the history of things (New Haven: Yale University Press, 2008) 83-96.
  • Keith Moxey, Visual Time: the image in history (Durham: Duke University Press, 2013), 11-22.
  • Fuyubi Nakamura, Morgan Perkins and Olivier Krischer, "Images of Asia across borders" in Asia through art and anthropology: cultural translation across borders, eds. Fuyubi Nakamura, Morgan Perkins and Olivier Krischer (London: Bloomsbury, 2013) 1-15.

Additional:

  • T.K. Sabapathy, "Developing regionalist perspectives in Southeast Asian Art Historiography (1996)" in Contemporary art in Asia: a critical reader, eds. Melissa Chiu and Benjamin Genocchio (Cambridge: MIT Press, 2011) 47-59.

A Working Bibliography:

  • John Clark, Bibliography of modern and contemporary art of Southeast Asia, 2015.
