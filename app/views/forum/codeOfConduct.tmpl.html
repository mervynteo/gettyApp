Forum Code of Conduct

Please help us create a healthy learning environment by respecting the following standards:

  • Be polite and respectful. Please respect your fellow colleagues. Insulting, condescending, or abusive words will not be condoned.
  • Be sensitive. Please be aware that everyone will have differing perspectives and it is important to be kind, thoughtful, and open-minded when discussing race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, or controversial topics.
  • Post appropriate content. You may not post inappropriate deemed pornographic or obscene, or advertise or promote outside products or organizations, or spam the forums with repeat content.

Posts that violate this Code will be deleted or made invisible by the forum moderator.
