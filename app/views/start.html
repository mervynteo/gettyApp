Videos

We will be posting video segments of the workshops held in Sydney, Singapore and Indonesia here. These videos are for private viewing only, and are not to be circulated or published in any other website.


Readings and Sources

Occasionally, we will be uploading critical readings ahead of a seminar or masterclass. Readings can be downloaded as a PDF and will be made available to the participant at least one week ahead of the masterclass.
